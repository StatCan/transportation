International travellers entering or returning to Canada, by type of transport

Release date: Mar 31, 2019

This flow diagram is This flow diagram is based on estimates from the Frontier Counts program. Data are produced using administrative data received from the Canada Border Services Agency (CBSA) on all international travellers who have been cleared for entry or re-entry into Canada. This includes residents of Canada, the United States and overseas entering Canada from abroad.
